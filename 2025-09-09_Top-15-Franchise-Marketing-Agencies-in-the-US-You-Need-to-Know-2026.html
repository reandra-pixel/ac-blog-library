How to Maximize Your Partnership with a Marketing Agency

  • Define clear objectives and KPIs
  • Maintain transparent, regular communication
  • Collaborate on content and strategy ideas
  • Track performance and stay open to adjustments

Budgeting for Franchise Marketing Services

  • Allocate 5–10% of gross revenue to marketing
  • Diversify spend across high-ROI channels like SEO, PPC, and social
  • Reserve budget for testing and contingency
  • Track ROI consistently to reallocate funds effectively
