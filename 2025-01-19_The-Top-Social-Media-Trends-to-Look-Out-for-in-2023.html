The Top Social Media Trends to Look Out for in 2023

Eden Jones  |  2025-01-19

Growing Your Social Media Presence In 2023. How To Stay On Top Of The Latest Content Marketing Trends

As we enter a new year, it’s important to stay on top of the latest social media trends to ensure that your brand’s online presence stays relevant and effective. In this article, we will explore some of the top social media trends to look out for in 2023.

1. Increased Use of Video Content

Video content has been on the rise for the past few years and this trend is only expected to continue in 2023. In fact, it is predicted that by 2023, 82% of all internet traffic will be video content. This means that it is more important than ever for brands to incorporate video into their social media strategies.

One way to do this is by using platforms like TikTok and Instagram Reels, which are specifically designed for short-form video content. These platforms allow brands to showcase their products and services in a fun and engaging way, while also building a connection with their audience.

2. The Continued Rise of Social Commerce

Social commerce, or the ability to purchase products directly through social media platforms, is expected to continue its upward trend in 2023. With the proliferation of shoppable posts and the increasing integration of payment options on social media platforms, it has never been easier for consumers to make a purchase without leaving their favorite app.

For brands, this means that it is more important than ever to have a strong presence on social media platforms, as well as to make sure that their products are easily accessible to their audience. Utilizing features like Instagram’s shoppable posts and Facebook’s Shop tab can help drive sales and increase brand visibility.

3. The Growing Importance of User-Generated Content

User-generated content, or content created by customers or followers, has the potential to be some of the most powerful content for a brand. This is because it is seen as more authentic and trustworthy by consumers, as it is coming from someone who has firsthand experience with the product or service.

In 2023, it is expected that brands will increasingly tap into the power of user-generated content by utilizing hashtags and running social media campaigns that encourage their audience to share their own content featuring the brand. This can help to increase brand awareness and build a stronger connection with the audience.

4. The Emergence of New Social Media Platforms

While it is impossible to predict exactly which new social media platforms will emerge in 2023, it is certain that there will be new players entering the market. It is important for brands to stay up-to-date on the latest platforms and consider whether or not they would be a good fit for their audience.

In addition to keeping an eye out for new platforms, it is also important for brands to stay flexible and be willing to adapt their social media strategies as needed. This could mean allocating resources to new platforms as they gain popularity, or shifting their focus away from platforms that are no longer as effective.

5. The Growing Importance of Social Media for Customer Service

In the digital age, it is more important than ever for brands to provide excellent customer service. With the proliferation of social media, it is likely that more and more customers will turn to these platforms to voice their complaints or ask for assistance.

In 2023, it is expected that brands will place a greater emphasis on their social media customer service efforts. This could include increasing the number of staff dedicated to monitoring and responding to customer inquiries on social media, as well as implementing chatbots and other automation tools
