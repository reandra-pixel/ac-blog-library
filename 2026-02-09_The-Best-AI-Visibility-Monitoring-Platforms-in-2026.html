What is AI visibility?

AI visibility means knowing whether your brand shows up when people ask AI tools for recommendations.

Instead of searching Google and clicking links, people now ask:

  • ChatGPT
  • Gemini
  • Claude
  • Perplexity
  • Google AI Overviews

AI visibility tools show you:

  • if your brand is mentioned
  • how often competitors are mentioned instead
  • and why AI is choosing certain brands as answers

Think of it as SEO for AI answers.

What to look for in an AI monitoring tool

Before spending money, make sure the tool can:

  • Track multiple AI platforms (not just ChatGPT)
  • Monitor brand mentions and citations
  • Show which prompts matter
  • Compare you vs competitors
  • Export reports easily
  • Give insights you can actually act on

If it only gives screenshots… walk away.

Top 10 AI Visibility Tools for 2026

1) RankPrompt – Best overall, especially for location-based visibility

Website: https://rankprompt.com

RankPrompt is built for how AI search is evolving, especially location-based answers.
AI recommendations change depending on where someone is asking from, and RankPrompt tracks that at country, city, and even ZIP-code level.

Best for:
Multi-location brands, franchises, agencies, and modern SEO teams.

2) LLM Pulse – Best for detailed AI response analysis & citation tracking

Website:https://llmpulse.ai/

LLM Pulse goes beyond simple visibility tracking. It shows not just whether your brand appears in AI answers, but why.

The platform tracks mentions, citations, share of voice, sentiment, and even the queries AI models use behind the scenes. It supports ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

Best for:
Agencies and marketing teams that want detailed breakdowns of AI responses and competitor comparisons, not just surface-level tracking.

3) Peec AI – Best for clean dashboards & competitor tracking

Website: https://www.peec.ai

Peec AI focuses on prompt tracking, visibility monitoring, and competitor comparisons, without overwhelming you with features you’ll never use.

Best for:
Mid-sized teams and agencies that want clarity and speed.

4) OtterlyAI – Best budget-friendly starter tool

Website: https://otterly.ai

OtterlyAI is easy to set up and easy to understand. It’s often the first AI visibility tool teams use to prove the value of AI tracking.

Best for:
Teams just getting started with AI visibility.

5) Hall – Best for quick, shareable reports

Website: https://usehall.com

Hall stands out for fast setup, simple dashboards, and shareable reports. It also offers a generous free entry point.

Best for:
Agencies and teams who want quick wins and easy reporting.

6) Scrunch – Best for monitoring + optimization

Website: https://scrunch.ai

Scrunch doesn’t just track AI visibility, it helps teams turn insights into actions, with optimization workflows and trend analysis.

Best for:
Teams that want to actively improve AI visibility, not just monitor it.

7) Semrush AI Visibility Toolkit – Best all-in-one SEO option

Website: https://www.semrush.com

If your team already uses Semrush, this add-on makes AI visibility part of your existing SEO workflow, including Google AI Overviews.

Traditional SEO teams expanding into AI search.

8) Rankscale – Best simple AI answer tracking

Website: https://rankscale.ai

Rankscale focuses on straightforward AI answer monitoring and competitor benchmarking, without enterprise complexity.

Best for:
Teams that want focused AI visibility tracking.

9) Writesonic – Best content + visibility combo

Website: https://writesonic.com

Writesonic blends AI visibility insights with content creation, helping teams see where they appear and create content to improve citations.

Best for:
Content-driven marketing teams.

10) Profound – Best for enterprise teams

Website: https://www.tryprofound.com

Profound is a premium, enterprise-focused platform designed for large brands that need deep reporting, advanced workflows, and executive-level insights.

How to choose the right tool

  • Choose RankPrompt → If location-based AI visibility matters
  • Choose LLM Pulse → If you want deep citation tracking
  • Choose Profound or Scrunch → If you’re enterprise-level
  • Choose Peec or Hall → If you want fast setup and clean dashboards
  • Choose Semrush → If you want AI visibility inside your SEO stack
  • Choose Otterly or Rankscale → If you’re testing the waters
Do I really need an AI visibility tool?
If people search for your services using ChatGPT or Google AI Overviews, then yes. If you don’t track it, you won’t know when competitors replace you in AI answers.
Is AI visibility the same as SEO?
Not exactly. SEO focuses on ranking websites. AI visibility focuses on being mentioned or cited inside AI-generated answers, even when no one clicks a link.
Can AI answers change by location?
Yes, big time. AI recommendations often change based on where the user is, which is why geo-level tracking is becoming essential.
Are these tools only for big companies?
No. There are tools here for startups, agencies, mid-sized teams, and enterprises. The key is choosing one that matches your budget and goals.
What’s the biggest mistake teams make with AI visibility?
Tracking AI answers… but not acting on them. The best tools don’t just show data, they help you fix why you’re not showing up.
